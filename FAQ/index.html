FAQs

1. I want to sign up online as a tutor, what now?
Complete your profile online at https://coachin.com before logging into the app. You can also sign up with LinkedIn and we will fetch all the required details from your LinkedIn profile. Log into the app with the same credentials you signed up with online.

2. After I sign up as a tutor, how do I accept sessions?
You can start taking sessions immediately after completing our signup process! Anytime a student requests for a session, we will check if he or she matches your major and other preferences. You'll receive a notification. Accepting the session is just a click from there!

3. After I sign up as a student, how do I accept sessions?
As per your preferences of subject, location and university, we will match you with the best rated tutor and send you a notification. Accepting the session is just a click from there!

4. How will students pay tutors?
Students will pay CoachIn and CoachIn will then pay the tutor.To make payment, just go to your profile to find different methods to pay.

5. How will a student get matched to a tutor?
CoachIn looks at the preferences of major, location, university,etc. entered by students and tutors and then finds the match which satisfies maximum preferences of both sides.
